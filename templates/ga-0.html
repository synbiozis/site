The project

This project is about comparing genetic algorithme as we use them today and genetic as it actually exist. Genetic algorithmes are based on natural selection but how they work is quite different, in many ways, including how to choose the bests individuals in a population or simple crossing overs.


A simple test

In order to test the different versions of my algorithme, I am gonna set a precise goal for it to reach. To be more precise, my populations will be initiated with random series of 0 and 1, each one representing a chromosome of an individual. I will define a solution, wich will be a unique serie of 0 and 1. The purpuse of my algorithme is to create individual unitl on is excactly equal to the solution. All along my tests, i will indicate how much generations a version require in order to reach its goal, and how much time the entire algoritme requires.
